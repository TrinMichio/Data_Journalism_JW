Take Aways

If I did this again, I would focus less on the CSS of one page, so I could have made every page look better.

Interpretations

The middle region of the United States has little housing units compared to the coasts.

The number of houses has been consistently increasing, indicating more people want houses.

The dataset is not as insightful, as I hoped. This is because states like Texas clearly have more houses due to its much larger land mass.
